# The Bell Family History

The private family-history site of the Bell family — nine generations of one
American family, from Virginia in 1776 to today.

**Visit: [bellfamilytree.com](https://bellfamilytree.com)**

The record is kept for the family: the site asks for the family password
(family members — it's in your letter or invitation email, or write us at
bellfamilytree250@gmail.com).

Built from *The Bell Family History* by **Mary Wiltse Heck** (September 1985) —
eight years of her letters, courthouse research, and interviews are the reason
this family's first two centuries survive — together with the 2026 research
that digitized her book, verified key facts against primary records, and
restored a missing branch of the family.

*This repository contains only the published page. The family's records are
maintained privately.*
